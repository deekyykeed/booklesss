## Free / Trial Students

**Model:** Free students get access as Slack single-channel guests (free on Business+, up to the guest ratio). They can see one course channel only — enough to see the value, not the full product.

**This is the funnel:** Lead magnet → Slack invite (free trial) → see quality of content → convert to paid.

Do not give free students full workspace access. One channel, one taste.
