# Booklesss — Brand Angle & Copy Positioning

## The Core Angle

**Booklesss is simply a workspace that makes it easier to be a student.**

That's it. No over-claiming. No transformation narrative. No "second brain."
Just: being a student is hard and disorganised — Booklesss makes it easier.

---

## What This Means for Copy

- Don't promise outcomes (passing, getting an A, acing exams)
- Don't be corny ("focus on your degree, not your notes")
- Don't over-explain the product
- Write like the template: short, confident, honest

The Prodo template energy is the right reference — keep its structure and tone.
Just change the angle from "professional productivity" to "student workspace."

---

## Template Reference

**Live template:** https://prodo.framer.website/

The Prodo template captures the right energy completely.
Adapt the angle, not the structure.

## Banned Words (Enforced)

tapestry, nuance, multifaceted, robust, delve, foster, Furthermore,
"It's worth noting", landscape, journey, empower, leverage (as verb),
game-changer, seamless, holistic, synergy
